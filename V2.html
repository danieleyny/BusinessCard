About Sharon

Sharon Eyny is the CEO of Ono International Academic College (Ono Friends) leading Ono’s global growth and partnerships. With extensive experience in strategic partnerships, business development, and global media, she works to connect education, innovation, and community impact.

Sharon is passionate about making higher education more inclusive and globally connected and she brings creativity, vision, and passion to advancing Ono’s international mission.
